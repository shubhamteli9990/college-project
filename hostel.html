Hostel Facilities

The Institute provides separate hostel facilities for both boys and girls. The hostels are well-furnished with A.C. and non-A.C. rooms. There is a provision of 24-hour power and water backup with wireless internet connectivity.

Our Hostels are Equipped With:
  • 24-hour electricity and water supply.
  • Hygienic and tasty mess facility.
  • Wi-Fi enabled computer facility.
  • Indoor games like chess, carom, and badminton.
  • 24-hour security with armed guards.
  • Laundry facility within the campus.
